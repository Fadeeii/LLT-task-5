GEC PALAKKAD!!

Government Engineering College Palakkad (GEC Palakkad), located in Sreekrishnapuram, is a center of technical excellence offering quality education under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. With its scenic campus, supportive community, and vibrant culture, it’s more than just an institution—it’s a place to grow and thrive. I love GEC Palakkad for its inspiring environment, skilled faculty, and opportunities that fuel both learning and creativity.
